Autonomous Irrigation System:

As a part of my independent study on the Internet of Things, I developed an autonomous irrigation system that utilizes a Raspberry Pi, soil sensors, and water pumps. The purpose of this system is to simplify the caretaking of indoor plants. When the soil sensor reads low, the system will automatically water the plant until the sensor reads adequate moisture again. This project also consisted of a local webserver to monitor the system. It provided settings to see when the plant was last watered, a reading of the current moisture level, and an ability to turn on/off autonomous watering.
